## KIN-034 — Noise Meter

**Version:** v1.0
**Category:** Education
**Creator:** Liam
**URL:** https://kin-034.kintools.me
**Privacy:** Nothing stored, nothing sent (stateless)

See when a room gets too loud. Green, yellow, red colour metering with adjustable sensitivity and screen wake lock. Built for classrooms — put a tablet at the front of the room and let the colour bar do the talking. Wake lock keeps the screen on automatically while listening. Sensitivity slider adapts to different microphone hardware.
