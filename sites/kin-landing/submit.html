The ethos

Kin tools are the antidote to subscription creep. Every tool in the catalog does one thing, works offline, and asks nothing in return. No upsell. No freemium wall.

If your idea fits that spirit — even if it is half-formed — send it. We would rather see an early idea that belongs here than a polished pitch for something that does not.
