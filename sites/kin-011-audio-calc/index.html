Phase Offset Calculator
Calculate the delay correction needed to bring two signals back into phase. Used when aligning microphones, fixing comb filtering, or time-aligning speakers.
Correction in samples — common rates
180° offset = complete phase inversion (polarity flip). 90° = quarter-cycle delay. In mixing, small phase errors between microphones create comb filtering and hollow tone.
